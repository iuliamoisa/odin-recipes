Pizza

Description

This is a classic homemade pizza recipe, including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions with photos. Make perfect pizza at home!

Ingredients

  • extra virgin olive oil
  • cornmeal
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • mushrooms
  • pesto
  • pepperoni
  • ham
  • onions

Steps

  1. proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. let the dough rise
  4. Preheat pizza stone (or pizza pan or baking sheet)
  5. Divide the dough into two balls
  6. Sprinkle pizza peel with corn meal, put flattened dough on top
  7. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  8. Sprinkle cornmeal on pizza stone, slide pizza onto pizza stone in oven
  9. Bake
